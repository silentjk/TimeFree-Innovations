Products

TimeFree is a virtual queuing system that creates an environment of positive customer experiences for establishments with high volume of customer transactions. With TimeFree, customers no longer have to physically fall in line or have to wait for their turn in the establishment. They can spend their wait time more productively since they can leave the establishment without losing their slot in the virtual queue and they will be alerted if their turn is about to come.

Establishments benefit in using the system since this helps them address their growing customer base without having to physically expand their stores, which is very expensive. Furthermore, it helps improve the overall customer experience since customers can do anything they want during their wait time without having to worry about missing their turn.

With the fierce competition in today's market, customer experience is the deciding factor in how your business attracts new customers, retains them, and builds customer loyalty. Fortifying your customer service with TimeFree can help your business achieve more sales by decreasing walk-away customers and ensuring customer engagements.
