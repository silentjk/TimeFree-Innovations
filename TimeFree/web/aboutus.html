About TimeFree Innovations

TimeFree Innovations is a virtual queuing solutions company that helps businesses improve its customer experience and enhance customer engagements, thus enabling it to attract new customers and build customer loyalty.

Founded in 2013, TimeFree Innovations has rolled out its virtual queuing solution in the Philippines and in Hong Kong. The company will be launching soon in Malaysia.

The company is backed by IdeaSpace Philippines, the incubator and accelerator arm of the MVP Group of Companies.
